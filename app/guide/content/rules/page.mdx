**Rules of Kem Belia**
<br/>
Leadership, at its core, is about setting direction, inspiring others, and achieving collective goals. While it may seem counterintuitive, rules play a pivotal role in shaping effective leadership. By providing structure, guidance, and a shared understanding of expectations, rules lay the foundation for leadership development and foster an environment conducive to growth and success.
<br/>
Here are some of the rules that you need to follow during your stay in Kem Belia:
<br/>
1.⁠ ⁠Tepati Masa\
2.⁠ ⁠⁠Usah berdua-duaan\
3.⁠ ⁠⁠Tumpu perhatian\
4.⁠ ⁠⁠Tidak mencarut\
5.⁠ ⁠⁠Ingat peraturan\
6.⁠ ⁠⁠Keterampilan seorang pemimpin

<p style={{marginLeft: '20px'}}> - Kuku sentiasa pendek/tidak berwarna</p>
<p style={{marginLeft: '20px'}}> - ⁠⁠Janggut dicukur kemas</p>
<p style={{marginLeft: '20px'}}> - R⁠ambut disikat rapi- lelaki</p>
<p style={{marginLeft: '20px'}}> - Rambut panjang diikat kemas- perempuan</p>
<p style={{marginLeft: '20px'}}> - Warna getah rambut- hitam/biru/putih</p>
<p style={{marginLeft: '20px'}}> - Seluar tidak singkat</p>
<p style={{marginLeft: '20px'}}> - Stokin hingga paras buku lali</p>
<p style={{marginLeft: '20px'}}> - Kasut bertutup</p>
7.⁠ ⁠⁠Lead by example(Be the example)\
8.⁠ ⁠⁠Respect everyone (yourself and each other)\
<p style={{marginLeft: '20px'}}> - Panggilan En/Cik/Tuan</p>
